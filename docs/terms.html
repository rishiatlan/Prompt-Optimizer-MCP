The Free tier includes 10 lifetime optimizations and access to all 19 tools. No account or payment information is required.

Paid tiers (Pro, Power, and Enterprise) are activated via Ed25519 license keys delivered after purchase. License keys are validated entirely offline — no license server, no network call, no account system. Payment terms and pricing are published separately on the pricing page.
